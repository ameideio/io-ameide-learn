# Consulting Partners

For firms delivering implementation, advisory, and managed services on
Ameide — engagement leads, delivery architects, consultants, and
partner admins.

This section mirrors the **Partner perspective** in the Ameide portal.

## What's covered

- **Sell** — Customers, pipeline, and offerings.
- **Deliver** — Engagement methodology and delivery patterns.
- **Service** — Service management and SLAs.
- **People** — Team capacity, skills, and performance.
- **Admin** — Partner accounts, contracts, and settings.

For the Ameide Partner program itself (tiers, certification, deal
registration), see [ameide.io/partners](https://ameide.io/partners).
