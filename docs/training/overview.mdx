# Training & Certifications

Build your skills on Ameide through guided learning paths, courses,
and credentials.

## What's covered

- **Learning paths** — Curated reading orders by role: Architect,
  Consultant, Project / Program Manager, Administrator.
- **Courses** — Standalone modules covering features and workflows.
- **Certifications** — Foundations, Architect, and Consultant credentials.
- **Playbooks** — End-to-end walkthroughs you can follow on your own data.

## Who this is for

Anyone who wants to go deeper than reference docs. Customer-org users,
partner consultants, and individuals preparing for an Ameide credential
all use the same paths.
